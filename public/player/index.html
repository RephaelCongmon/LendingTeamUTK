What is this site?

https://brawlhallastats.com is a website for Brawlhalla stats that allows players to search up and analyze a wide variety of Brawlhalla statistics for their own player account or others. You can easily retrieve insightful information about your overall performance, ranked games, and opponent information. Some cool features include:
  • Total damage dealt and taken statistics (by category and legends too!).
  • Sortable and searchable legends table that displays a variety of statistics per legend of that player.
  • Easy access to the player's clan statistics.
How to Use this Site?
There are two simple ways to look up your statistics. You can either search by player name and click on a search result, or you can directly look up by Brawlhalla ID (which is found on the top-right corner in-game inside the inventory panel).
Future Updates
One tool that I am developing right now is a compare players application where you can look up two people by player name or ID, and then compare the two player's statistics. Keep checking back in or follow my Twitter for more updates!
