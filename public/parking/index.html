    What is Scooter Parking

    This map displays high volume clusters where the routes of scooters are determined to have started or ended. By using public scooter data in conjuction with Google APIs we were able to map sectors where scooter route destinations were most concentrated. Try zooming in and out using the plus and minus icons to the right! Conclusions drawn from this data include:
    • Mappings to solve issues of scooter parking locations
    • Points of interest that contain high scooter traffic
    • Area where scooters should likely be moved to for optimal use
