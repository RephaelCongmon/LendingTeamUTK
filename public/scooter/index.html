    What is the Scooter Heatmap

    This heat map displays high volume transit routes of scooters and gives insights to high-traffic areas. By using public scooter data in conjuction with the Google Map API we were able to chart popular scooter routes of interest. Conclusions drawn from this data include:
    • What routes do knoxvillians enjoy scooting the most
    • What routes are dead spots for scooter traffic and why that might be
    • What areas could we implement more for scooter safety
