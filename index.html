    What is this site?

    https://brawlhallastats.com is a website tracker for Brawlhalla stats that allows players to search up, track and analyze a wide variety of Brawlhalla statistics for their own player account or others. You can easily retrieve insightful information about your overall performance, ranked games, leaderboards and opponent information. Some cool features include:
    • Total damage dealt and taken statistics (by category and legends too!).
    • Sortable and searchable legends table that displays a variety of statistics per legend of that player.
    • Easy access to the player's clan statistics.
    • Works for all systems (including PS4, Nintendo Switch, Xbox)
    • Global statistics added across all players that exist on this tracker. Visit the totals page!
    How to Use this Site?
    There are two simple ways to look up your statistics. You can either search by player name and click on a search result, or you can directly look up by Brawlhalla ID (which is found on the top-right corner in-game inside the inventory panel).
    Note: If you have not played a Ranked 1v1 match in the current season, you will not show up if you search by Brawlhalla Name. Search by Brawlhalla ID.
    Recent Updates
    • Lots of Data Table enhancements. You can now scroll through the Data Table for the different columns. You can also reorder the columns by clicking and dragging.
    • I am removing the long list of legend data when you search up a player and instead putting that data inside the data table. It is easier to read and is more user-friendly.
    • Names from the 1v1, 2v2, and Most Searched Leaderboard are now clickable to directly view the player's stats!
    Future Updates
    One tool that I am developing right now is a compare players application where you can look up two people by player name or ID, and then compare the two player's statistics. Keep checking back in or follow my Twitter for more updates!
